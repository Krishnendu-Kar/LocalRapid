Replacement & Exchange Policy

Fast — Fair — Friendly. If an item arrives damaged, defective, wrong, or incomplete, choose a replacement of the same item or an exchange for a product of equal value.

What this covers

Damaged items, wrong deliveries, and missing parts. Not for buyer-changed-mind returns.

How long you have

Report within 48 hours from delivery with photos or video. We’ll respond within 1-2 business days.

Processing time

After verification, replacements/exchanges are dispatched within 3–5 business days (may vary by seller/location).

Quick Steps to Request a Replacement or Exchange

Step 1 — Report: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with order number, brief description and at least one photo or short video showing the issue through WhatsApp.
Step 2 — Verification: Our team reviews the evidence and may request additional pictures or a short inspection note from the delivery partner.
Step 3 — Confirm: Once verified, you’ll receive an email/SMS/message with options: Replacement (same item) or Exchange (item of equal value). Choose your preferred option.
Step 4 — Pickup & Dispatch: If a pickup is required, schedule it in the WhatsApp chat. Replacement or exchanged item is dispatched after receiving or confirming the return.
Step 5 — Tracking: We’ll share tracking information. Delivery times depend on shop & courier availability.

Eligibility & Requirements

  • The issue must be reported within 48 hours of delivery acceptance.
  • Provide clear photos or video showing damage, defect, or wrong item.
  • Items must be returned in original packaging (when possible) with accessories and manuals.
  • Products that show signs of misuse, alteration, or physical damage after delivery may be declined.
  • Certain categories (staples) might have special rules. See Exceptions below.

Exceptions & Special Cases

Some items require additional steps or cannot be exchanged due to hygiene or safety rules. Examples:

  • Staples — Only eligible if delivered spoiled or damaged and reported immediately.

Can I exchange for a higher value item?

+

Yes — you can request a higher-value exchange. The price difference must be paid by the customer at checkout. Our support team will provide a secure payment link.

What if my replacement is out of stock?

+

If the same item is not available, you can choose an exchange of equal value or request a full refund. We’ll prioritize a timely resolution.

Do I pay for return shipping?

+

As the return is due to our error (damaged/defective/wrong item), return shipping is free.
