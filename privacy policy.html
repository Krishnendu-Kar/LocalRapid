Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us — here’s how we protect it at LocaalRapid

1. Introduction

At LocaalRapid, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you make a purchase from our website:

  • Personal Details: Name, contact number, shipping/billing address, and email (optional).
  • Payment Information: Payment method, transaction ID, and billing details.
  • Communication Data: Messages or inquiries sent through WhatsApp or our support form.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Provide customer support and updates regarding your purchase.
  • Improve our website experience and services.
  • Send promotional offers and updates (only with your consent).

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures — including SSL encryption, secured payment, and strict internal data policies — to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your data.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data to anyone. However, we may share necessary information with:

  • Delivery and logistics partners for order shipment.
  • Payment service providers for transaction processing.
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to prevent fraud.

6. Local Storage & Browser Data

Our website uses your browser's local storage to save certain settings and improve your experience. No personal information is stored in cookies, and we do not track your browsing behavior outside of your interactions with our site.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and review your personal information.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of promotional emails or messages at any time.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected — typically until your order and after-sales services are completed.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website indicates acceptance of the revised terms.
